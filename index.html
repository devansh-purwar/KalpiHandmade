Our Process

Each sheet of our handmade paper is produced using traditional techniques that date back generations, often involving minimal mechanization. The paper is made from a range of raw materials, primarily recycled cotton rags, agricultural waste, and sometimes bamboo or mulberry bark. The process involves pulping the fibers, pressing them into sheets, and drying them in natural sunlight, lending the paper a distinct texture and durability..
