how it's made

our craft, step by step

each sheet tells a story of tradition, skill, and respect for nature.

01

source & pulp

We collect recycled cotton rags, agricultural waste, and natural fibers, then transform them into a fine pulp using traditional methods.

02

form & press

Skilled artisans pour the pulp onto mesh frames, evenly spreading each sheet by hand before pressing out excess water.

03

dry & finish

Sheets are dried in natural sunlight, giving each piece its distinctive texture, durability, and organic character.
